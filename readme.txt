******** Readme for nanotribo data**************
Nanoscale friction measurements of thin films samples: Al, Al2O3, MoS2, TiO2 and SS (stainless steel) made via Lateral force microscopy.
Free to use for modelling, machine learning, AI purposes, as slong as the following papers are cited:

***************************************************
Perčić, M., Zelenika, S., Mezić, I. et al. An experimental methodology for the concurrent characterization of multiple parameters influencing nanoscale friction. Friction 8, 577–593 (2020). https://doi.org/10.1007/s40544-019-0289-z

Perčić, M., Zelenika, S. & Mezić, I. Artificial intelligence-based predictive model of nanoscale friction using experimental data. Friction (2021). https://doi.org/10.1007/s40544-021-0493-5
***************************************************

Short description:

CVT data - Centroidal Voronio Tesselation main experimental dataset 50 measurement points with 5 repetitions, measurements of Ff (nanoscale friction force in nN), with variable sliding velocity (v), normal force (Fn), adhesion (Fa), and temperature (t)

TD data - Monte Carlo test dataset 15 measurement points with 5 repetitions

ranges of variables:
Fn = 10 to 150 nN
v = 5 to 500 nm/s
t = 20 to 80 °C

Ff nanoscale friction force (nN)
Fa nanoscale adhesion force (nN)
Fna sum of normal force Fn and adhesion force Fa
Both datasets provided for separate 5 measurement repetitions and mean values in respective directories.
